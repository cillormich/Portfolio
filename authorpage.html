Councillor Onyeama - Author

Councillor Onyeama has always wanted to write a book that will communicate and reveal the unadulterated truth and basic realities about life, which will, in turn, teach the reader how best to live life and survive in a complex and highly competitive world. To write his first book, he was for nearly 10 years dedicated to studying life, people, the world, and human behaviour. This study gave rise to YOUR PERSON IN A WORLD OF PEOPLE. Within the years of research and writing of the book, he has worked as a reporter for a state broadcasting corporation as well as an office and teaching assistant in two Nigerian universities. He has a Degree in Linguistics and Communication Studies. He strongly believes that the best knowledge anyone can have - especially one for winning and survival - is knowledge about people. According to him, if we do not understand people and by extension, the world, we will for sure not be able to know how to succeed, win, survive, and even make our lives better. He bases his research and writing on these premises and writes books that teach same.

YOUR PERSON IN A WORLD OF PEOPLE

Your One and Only ULTIMATE GUIDE for Winning & Survival

BOOKS BY COUNCILLOR ONYEAMA

YOUR PERSON IN A WORLD OF PEOPLE is Councillor Onyeama's first published book. A work of 10 years of research and 3 years of writing often described as the reader's ULTIMATE GUIDE for winning and survival. The book is about life, the world, people, winning and survival. It covers key issues that matter most in everyone’s life. It presents to you the world and people exactly the way they are, the same way it helps you to transform into a more rational and strategic person. And to everyone who is constantly troubled by all the topsy-turviness that life brings, this book gives the needed sense of direction in a complex and highly competitive world. It is the only ammunition you’ve got.
